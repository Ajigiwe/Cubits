## Brand & Style

This design system is tailored for a highly professional premium interior design studio (Cubits & Co. Interiors). It shifts the visual identity back to a rich dark charcoal (#2C2825) and warm linen/cream (#E8E0D5) contrast with thin, vertical/horizontal gold rule accents.

## Colors

- **Deep Warm Charcoal (#2C2825):** Core canvas background for dark sections.
- **Warm Linen/Cream (#E8E0D5):** Clean background surface for light sections.
- **Muted Gold/Caramel (#C4A882):** Highlight accents and rule lines.
