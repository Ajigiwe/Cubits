About The Studio

Purity. Space.
Architectural Craft.

We reject cluttered decoration in favor of structural clarity, allowing light and natural materials to dictate the atmosphere of the room.

Our Design Pillars

Our philosophy is grounded in creating environments that achieve a harmonious balance between spatial purity and the textures of natural life. By curating custom millwork, raw mineral elements, and soft textiles, we craft bespoke sanctuaries that feel both deeply luxurious and organically grounded.

Spatial Purism

Structural honesty dictates the layouts, maximizing natural light and creating quiet spatial sightlines.

Tactile Integrity

Natural oak, travertine stone, and organic linen fibers are selected to age gracefully and speak directly to the senses.

Kofi Mensah

Principal Designer & Founder

With over a decade of design experience in luxury residential spaces and custom woodworks, Kofi Mensah directs the spatial orientation and tactile design guidelines for Interiors By Cubits Time.

"We believe that a space must tell the story of its materials. We don't construct designs; we curate sensory atmospheres where structural form and texture are at peace."
